About member for this project

Tanaka Laboratory is one of the laboratories of Tokai University in Japan. We investigate on space debris, aerospace traffic management, and image analysis of space debris. This project consists of four volunteers of Tanaka Laboratory. This web site focus on the visualization of COVID-19 data. We started to visualize "the current state" using basic techniques in Tanaka Laboratory because we heap blame on biased reporting and inaccurate aggregated data. We launched this site to have a good grasp of the situation of COVID-19. We are happy if it helps you.
